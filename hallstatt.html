AFTERGROUND

Location: Hallstatt, Austria Type: Graduation Project

Designed as a landslide-resilient settlement located inside a mountain chasm. Responding to local salt mining risks, the project utilizes a structural "zipper" concept to connect and stabilize the rock faces, creating habitable spaces within extreme topologies.
