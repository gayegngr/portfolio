About

Hello, I am a fourth-year architecture student at Izmir Institute of Technology (IZTECH). My work is deeply focused on adaptive reuse, heritage conservation, computational design, and sustainable spatial strategies.

I am particularly interested in how architecture can respond to complex environmental challenges, from rising sea levels to structural resilience in extreme topologies. My design process relies heavily on advanced 3D modeling, detailed structural diagramming, and creating high-quality architectural visualizations to communicate spatial narratives.
