Urban Studies and Design Center

The Urban Studies and Design Center is conceived as a civic hub that strengthens the relationship between architecture and the city. Shaped by the surrounding urban axes, the project responds to its context while creating a vibrant public destination.

The building is organized into two interconnected volumes. One accommodates public functions—including a library, auditorium, and multipurpose spaces—encouraging learning, cultural exchange, and community engagement. The other houses the İzmir Planning Agency, providing a flexible working environment while maintaining a balanced relationship between openness and privacy.

At ground level, cafés, exhibition spaces, and public gathering areas extend the life of the adjacent urban square, creating a seamless transition between the city and the building. By combining civic, cultural, and institutional programs, the project offers an inclusive and dynamic environment that enriches everyday urban life.
