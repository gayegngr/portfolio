Wildlife Research Center

Located within a biologically rich landscape, this research center is designed to support wildlife conservation while strengthening the relationship between people and nature. Beyond its research facilities, the project includes workshops, a library, exhibition spaces, a conference hall, and accommodation for both researchers and visiting guests.

Positioned on the site's steepest slope, the building captures panoramic sea views while carefully adapting to the natural terrain. Sustainability and environmental sensitivity guide every aspect of the design, creating a low-impact architecture that responds to its surroundings.

More than a scientific facility, the project serves as a platform for education, collaboration, and public engagement, encouraging cultural exchange and fostering a deeper awareness of the region's unique ecological heritage.
