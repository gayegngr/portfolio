About

Designing spaces that connect people, context and time.

I am an architect passionate about creating thoughtful, functional and timeless spaces. My interests focus on adaptive reuse, residential architecture, heritage conservation and computational design, combining creativity with technical precision throughout every stage of the design process.

I am particularly interested in private residential architecture, believing that homes should respond not only to function but also to atmosphere, identity and the everyday experiences of their users.

Open to innovation and continuous learning, I enjoy exploring new technologies, sustainable approaches and contemporary design methodologies that expand architectural possibilities.
