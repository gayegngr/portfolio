About

Designing spaces that connect people, place and time.

I am an architect with a strong interest in residential architecture, adaptive reuse, heritage conservation and computational design. My approach combines analytical thinking, spatial sensitivity, and a contemporary design language to create meaningful environments.

Through academic projects, architectural competitions and professional experience, I have developed skills in concept generation, architectural visualization, technical drawing, and digital workflows. I enjoy transforming complex design challenges into clear, functional and elegant architectural solutions.

I am continuously exploring innovative design methods, AI-assisted workflows, and sustainable architectural strategies while remaining open to learning new technologies and collaborating within international design teams.
