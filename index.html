About

Creating architecture that responds to people, place and time.

I am an architect with a strong interest in residential architecture, adaptive reuse, heritage conservation and computational design. My work combines conceptual thinking with technical precision to create meaningful spatial experiences.

I enjoy designing spaces that are both functional and emotionally engaging. Through research, digital tools and visualization, I aim to develop architecture that balances innovation with context.

Currently, I am seeking opportunities to contribute to international architectural practice while continuing to explore sustainable and human-centered design.
