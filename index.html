Term Design I

I am an architect passionate about designing thoughtful and enduring spaces that enrich everyday life. My interests lie in adaptive reuse, residential architecture, heritage conservation, and computational design, where research, creativity, and technical precision come together to shape meaningful environments.

I am particularly interested in private residential architecture, believing that homes should respond not only to function but also to emotion, identity, and the way people experience space. Curious by nature and open to innovation, I continuously explore emerging technologies, sustainable strategies, and new design methodologies to broaden my architectural perspective.

Whether developing conceptual ideas or technical solutions, I strive to create architecture that is contextual, timeless, and human-centered.

Design Philosophy

Architecture should never exist in isolation. It should belong to its place, respond to its users, and remain meaningful over time. My approach seeks clarity, simplicity, and innovation while creating spaces that quietly improve everyday life.
